Project Highlights (In Progress)

  • Led a team of 5 to design a 30lb combat robot using Onshape and CAM tools
  • Manufactured components via CNC machining, and Waterjet and ensured precise tolerances for performance
  • Developed custom armor solutions using 3D printed materials
  • Created and optimized drivetrain, weapon systems, and motors for reliable mechanical and electrical performance
  • Created comprehensive CAD models and technical documentation
  • Conducted stress-strain analysis in ANSYS to validate the structural integrity of components under combat conditions, ensuring durability and optimal material selection
  • Successfully competed in an NHRL event
